Gherkin

Gherkin is a business readable, domain specific language that allows you describe the software behaviour. It was introduced by cucumber (or jbehave).

Actually, as I understand it, the usage of the name “Gherkin” is a joke: The gherkin is a fruit similar in form and nutritional value to a cucumber. … (according to wikipedia:Gherkin).
